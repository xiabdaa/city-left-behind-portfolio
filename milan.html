01 / Modern cover

Milan

Milan appears as a city of inherited facades interrupted by transit, glass, illuminated signs, and surfaces made for display. The old city is still visible, but it is no longer alone.

Reading

The sequence begins with advertising and instruction, then moves into glass towers and construction. Milan is shown as a city where the present keeps building a surface over older stone, streets, and bodies.

Key feeling

Not nostalgia, but displacement. The old city remains present, yet every sign, camera, and new facade asks the viewer to look somewhere else.
