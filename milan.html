Milan

Milan appears as a city of inherited facades interrupted by transit, glass, illuminated signs, and surfaces made for display. The old city is still visible, but it is no longer alone.

Reading

The photographs look for the pressure of the present: light boxes, windows, graffiti, signage, and the isolated body moving through spaces that feel already arranged.

Key feeling

Not nostalgia, but displacement. Milan becomes a surface where memory is edited by modern life.
